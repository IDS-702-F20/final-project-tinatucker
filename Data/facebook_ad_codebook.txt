#Variable Codebook [Tina Tucker]

ad_count		Number of Facebook ads run in 4-month period prior to Election Day
dollar_spent		Dollar amount spent on Facebook ads in 4-month period prior to Election Day
year			Year of election (2018 or 2020)
candidate		Senate candidate name
senate_race		Senate race name
state			State of Senate race
region			Region of Senate race - northeast, midwest, southeast, southwest, and west
special			Special or General Election
party			Political party - Republican or Democratic
incumbent		Incumbent, challenger, or open seat
rating			Competitiveness rating from Cook Political Report
outcome			Election outcome - win, lose, or runoff
vote_pct		Vote share
vote_margin		Vote difference between candidate and opponent
